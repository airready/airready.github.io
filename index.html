Platform Capabilities

Advanced planning intelligence,
built for pilots.

📋

NOTAM Briefing

Pulls the official NOTAMs for your departure, destination and alternates into a single readable list, with a direct link to ENAIRE Insignia for the full official set.

⛅

Decoded Weather

Decodes live METARs into clear alerts for ceiling, visibility and VFR / MVFR / IFR category, and shows the official TAF alongside for the full forecast window.

💨

Automatic Crosswind Check

For each active runway, AirReady automatically computes the wind component against your aircraft's AFM demonstrated crosswind limit and flags any exceedance directly beside the live METAR.

🧭

Geodesic NavLog & Fuel

Great-circle route legs with dynamic TOC/TOD, per-leg TAS, ground speed and fuel, primary & diversion navlogs, and an operational fuel policy computed against your aircraft's real AFM performance.

📡

Live Weather Radar

Switch on a live precipitation radar overlay (RainViewer) directly on your route map, alongside VFR topographic and satellite base layers.

☁️

Cloud History

Every briefing you generate is saved to your account history, so you can revisit recent routes and dispatch records across sessions.

DISCLAIMER: AirReady is a free, non-operational study and cross-check aid for VFR general aviation. It is NOT approved for operational use and must never be the sole basis for a flight. It combines live third-party data with AirReady advisory calculations (headings, distances, times, fuel, levels, alternates and airspace warnings), which are estimates, are NOT official data and may contain errors. When a source is unreachable, AirReady shows a notice instead of inventing data. The Pilot in Command retains full legal responsibility to obtain and cross-check a complete official briefing (ENAIRE, AENA, AEMET, EASA) before any flight.
